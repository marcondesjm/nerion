## Description

NerionOS is a product design and technology studio focused on helping SaaS companies, startups, founders, and product teams create high-conversion digital experiences. The company works from brand and product design through implementation, combining business strategy, UX/UI, software development, integrations, automations, APIs, AI agents, and ongoing support.

Core positioning: "Colocamos seu SaaS na mesma linha dos gigantes."

## Methodology

The company presents the NERION method, with six stages:

1. **Nerion:** discovery of business, market, users, and competitors.
2. **Engage:** alignment of vision, expectations, references, benchmarks, and scope.
3. **Requirements:** definition and prioritization of features, integrations, and business rules.
4. **Interface:** high-fidelity prototyping in Figma with UX, UI, and microinteractions.
5. **Operate:** implementation of the functional product with modern and scalable technologies.
6. **Nurture:** post-launch measurement, optimization, iteration, and continuous improvement.
